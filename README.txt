The Android Automotive app connects your smart home directly to your vehicle. By acting as a secure relay, it allows you to safely control and monitor your smart home devices right from your car's dashboard without needing complex network configurations.

Experience the convenience of opening your garage door, turning on the driveway lights, or adjusting the thermostat as you arrive home, all without taking your hands off the wheel. The companion app ensures a seamless authentication flow, keeping your data secure and your focus on the road.
